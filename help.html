Instructions

BrewFormer is easy to use. In order to properly search for breweries:

(1) Fill in the "Region", which refers to a State which exists in the United States of America.

(2) Fill in the "City", wich refers to a city which exists in the state that you have entered into the region field

(3) Finally click the button labeled "Search" to initiate the brewery search process.

(The time it will take for the results to display is determined by how many breweries are found in the searched location. Please be patient.)

When the results finish loading up, you will see that each brewery is displayed in its own card. As well, you will see a list of beers listed under their respective breweries. If you hover over each beer a description for the that beer will appear. If you click on the beer, you will be sent to a google search result for said beer where you can continue researching the brew.
